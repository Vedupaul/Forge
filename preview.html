FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Everything needed to know before building your first website with AI.

Do I need design or coding skills?

No. Forge starts with natural language and keeps the generated code visible for teams that want control.

How does the AI builder work?

Planner, UI, component, styling, and debugging agents collaborate through one streaming workflow.

Can I export the code?

Yes. Projects can be exported as ZIPs, synced to GitHub, or prepared for Vercel deployment.

Is the backend ready?

This preview focuses on the frontend. Auth, Prisma, OpenAI, GitHub, and Vercel wiring comes next.
